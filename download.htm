Monet in Detail

Spend a Day with Claude Monet and Discover his Artistic Legacy

Discover the world of the Impressionist master Claude Monet on this exclusive day trip that delves deep into his life, work, and enduring legacy. With a private guide, chauffeured transportation, and skip-the-line tickets, you’ll embark on a journey through the picturesque landscapes and artistic inspiration that fueled Monet’s creativity.

House and Garden of Monet in Giverny – A Glimpse into Monet’s World

Your adventure begins as you’re comfortably transported to Giverny, where Claude Monet lived and painted for over four decades. Explore Monet’s iconic pink house, adorned with vibrant colors, and venture into his exquisite gardens, which served as a living canvas for his renowned Water Lilies series. Witness the Japanese Bridge and lily ponds that inspired some of his most celebrated works.

The Most Beautiful Villages of the Seine Valley – Impressionist Inspiration

Continue your journey through the enchanting Seine Valley, visiting the very villages that captivated Monet and other Impressionist painters. Stroll along charming streets, discover picturesque landscapes, and feel the artistic allure that has drawn artists to this region for centuries.

Orangerie Museum – Gazing at Monet’s Water Lilies Panels

Conclude your day with a private tour of the Orangerie Museum in Paris, where you’ll have the privilege of gazing at Monet’s stunning Water Lilies panels. These massive and immersive artworks are a testament to Monet’s genius, offering a serene and captivating visual experience that captures the essence of his artistic vision.
